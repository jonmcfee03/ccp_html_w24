Bangkok

Primary Image of Bangkok as a gif

Primary image of Bangkok as non-gif

Geographical Location: Southeast Asia

Bangkok, Thailand's capital, is a large city known for ornate shrines and vibrant street life. It is officially known in Thai as Krung Thep Maha Nakhon and colloquially as Krung Thep.

Bangkok welcomes more visitors than any other city in the world and it doesn’t take long to realize why. Bangkok is a city of contrasts with action at every turn. Marvel at the gleaming temples, catch a tuk tuk through bustling Chinatown or take a longtail boat through floating markets. Food is another Bangkok highlight, from local dishes served at humble street stalls to haute cuisine at romantic rooftop restaurants.

Khao San road, which now represents a series of streets, is a place in downtown Bangkok where backpackers and tourists are immediately drawn to from when they first touch down in the airport.

Lisbon

Animated image of a traditional Lisbon streetcar.

Photograph of a yellow tram in Lisbon.

Geographical Location: Europe

Lisbon, the capital city of Portugal, is renowned for its historical significance, hilly landscape, and iconic yellow trams. Known as Lisboa in Portuguese, this city is a blend of traditional architecture and contemporary culture, making it a popular destination in Europe.

As a coastal city, Lisbon boasts stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean, complemented by its picturesque streets and vibrant nightlife. The city's rich maritime history is evident in its historic sites, like the Belem Tower and the Jeronimos Monastery, and its culinary scene is highlighted by the famous Portuguese custard tarts, known as 'pastéis de nata'.

Visakhapatnam (Vizag)

Scenic view of Visakhapatnam, showcasing the city's urban landscape.

Geographical Location: Asia

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. The main language spoken here is Telugu, a Dravidian language. Vizag is known for its significant role in the Indian economy, its rich history, and its beautiful coastal line.
